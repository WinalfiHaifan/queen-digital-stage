About Queen

The band behind the stage.

A focused profile of Queen, their classic lineup, and the legacy behind their music and performances.

Overview

Rock, theatre, harmony, and scale.

Queen was formed in London in 1970 and became known for bold vocal arrangements, theatrical stage energy, and genre-blending rock songs. This page introduces Queen’s identity, classic members, key facts, and legacy before visitors explore the music and history in detail.
